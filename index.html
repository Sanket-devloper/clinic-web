Dr. Prerna Golekar Gopale is a dedicated and experienced clinical nutritionist with 12 years of experience and a passion for helping individuals achieve optimal health through personalized nutrition plans. She holds BAMS, PGDEMS and a Diploma in Clinical Nutrition.
