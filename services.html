Weight Loss Program

Most Popular

Our Weight Loss program is designed using evidence-based nutrition science to help you shed excess weight safely and sustainably. Dr. Prerna creates customized calorie-deficit plans based on your TDEE, BMI, and lifestyle.

Unlike crash diets that lead to rebound weight gain, our approach focuses on metabolic correction and sustainable eating habits. We don't believe in starvation — we believe in eating right.

Specialized
