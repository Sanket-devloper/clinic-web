Common Questions

Once you initiate the fees, we will call you and take your detailed history. Your daily routine will be assessed, and according to your TDEE and BMI, a personalized diet plan will be created. We will then call and explain the plan in detail — you can ask any queries you have. You'll send each meal pic for portion tracking, and update your progress every week to receive a new adjusted diet plan.

After reaching your target, if you start to eat everything without control, you may gain weight. That's why we provide a maintenance plan included in our program — so you can maintain your results long-term with sustainable eating habits.

We believe in the natural way to be healthy. We do not recommend any powders or medicines. Our approach focuses entirely on real food, balanced nutrition, and lifestyle changes for sustainable results.
